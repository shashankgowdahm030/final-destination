Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments via credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), UPI, PayPal, Net Banking, and other secure online payment gateways.
2. How can I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email or SMS with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order through our website or the courier partner’s tracking portal.
3. What is your return policy?
We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you may return the item within 5 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please refer to our Return & Exchange Policy page for more information.
4. Are the products on your website authentic?
Yes. All our products are 100% authentic. We source directly from authorized distributors and trusted manufacturers to ensure quality and originality.
5. What if my item is damaged during shipping?
In the rare case that your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will arrange a replacement or initiate a full refund as quickly as possible.
6. Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?
Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery for selected products and locations. COD eligibility will be shown at checkout.
7. How long will it take to receive my order?
Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days and delivered within 3–7 business days, depending on your location and the courier service.
