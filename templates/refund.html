Refund Policy

We have a 30-day return policy, which means you have 30 days after receiving your item to request a return.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it—unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. We will pick up the product within 3–5 business days. You’ll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

To start a return, you can contact us at support@rufitrrendz.online.

If your return is accepted, we’ll send you a return shipping label, as well as instructions on how and where to send your package. Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

You can always contact us for any return question at support@rufitrrendz.online.

Defects, Damages & Exchanges

  • If you receive a damaged or defective item, please notify us within 24–48 hours of receiving your package. We will require photos of the defects or damage in your return/refund request.
  • After approval, we will pick up the product within 3–5 business days.
  • To initiate a refund request, please email support@rufitrrendz.online with "Defect / Damage" in the subject line. Please include your name, order number, and a photo of the defect/damage (if applicable).
  • Please note that we do not offer refunds for items that have been used, opened, or tampered with in any way.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment.
  • We reserve the right to reject a refund request if we suspect abuse of our return policy.

European Union 14-Day Cooling Off Period

Notwithstanding the above, if the merchandise is being shipped into the European Union, you have the right to cancel or return your order within 14 days, for any reason and without a justification. As above, your item must be in the same condition that you received it—unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You’ll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

Refunds

We will notify you once we’ve received and inspected your return, and let you know if the refund was approved or not. If approved, you’ll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 10 business days.

Please remember it can take some time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund too. If more than 15 business days have passed since we’ve approved your return, please contact us at support@rufitrrendz.online.
